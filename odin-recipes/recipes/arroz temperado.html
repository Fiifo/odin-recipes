Season's rice

rice with a bunch of other stuffs to make it tasty, healthy and colorful! Recommend to eat it with rice recipe

ingredients:

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 pack of parsley
  • 1 pack of scallion
  • 1 pack of shredded olives
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 can of peas
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 piece of sliced peperoni

Steps

  1. Cook the rice normally, and put it aside for later
  2. In a pan, add a little of oil, the sliced onion and the peperoni, let it simmer until its golden-ish
  3. add the rest of the ingredients and let it saut√©, remember to slice all of them into cubes or slices!
  4. after its done add all of it to the rice and mix it all well!
